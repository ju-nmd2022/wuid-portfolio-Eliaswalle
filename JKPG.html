    • What can you do here?

  • HV-71: If you are interested in sport a truly suggest that you take the time and buying a ticket to go watch HV-71. They just made into the highest league this year and need your support, When you are there you will not just enjoy the atmosphere of Swedish hockey but also experince a live sports night in Sweden.
  • Habo Church: An astonishing sight, the Habo Church has the dimensions of a cathedral but is made completely from wood.
  • Jönköpings Citypark: West of the city centre, the city park is not your average city park; it’s more of a green city district with an array of facilities and attractions amid an arboretum planted in the year 1900. Jönköping’s open-air museum is here, and boasts ten historic buildings moved to the park to avoid demolition.
  • Tändsticksområdet: Tändsticksområdet is a beautiful and historical place to visit and can be explored all year around. In this area you can find stores and restaurants with high quality. You can find the highly praised restaurants like Pescadores, Restaurant Tegel and the beer bar Idlewild. You can walk in the store Atleljen, a place for events, photos and conference. In the heart of Tändsticksområdet you can locate the museum of matchsticks, 1 of 3 museums that tell the story about matchsticks in the world.
